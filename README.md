## Requirements

- macOS 15.0 or later
- Claude Code CLI or Codex CLI installed

## Privacy & Data (Summary)

- Commander is a local Mac app. Your credentials stay in your CLI, and your repo stays on your machine.
- Commander does not collect, store, or transmit your repositories to Commander servers.
- Your chosen CLI may send code context to its provider to generate responses.
